Christianity

I am a Christian. That means that I believe that Jesus Christ is the Son of God, that He was killed, and that He was raised from the dead so that we who believe in him, will be forgiven of our sins.

This does not make me superior. Christians are not better than non-christians. We merely accepted the gift that God gave us. We have nothing to brag about, and most of us don't claim to be without sin. We all sin every day of our lives. And that is the point behind the gift of salvation: We are sinners, and there is nothing we can do to make ourselves look good in God's eyes. We need Jesus Christ.
