Intro

In this essay I will not attempt to prove that God exists because is not possible to prove that God does in fact exist. Instead, I will attempt to prove that it is impossible for God not to exist. I will do this by asking four fairly complex questions and briefly discussing them. I will not however, throw the book at you. There will not be multiple references to the Bible, or to other works. These thoughts, come from my own head. I caution you, that inspite of lack of references, do not discard this essay for it might just make you think.
